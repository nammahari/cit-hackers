# B.E. Mechanical Engineering Curriculum (R2024)
## Chennai Institute of Technology

### 1. Credit Definition
The credit system is structured based on weekly contact hours:
* **1 Hour Lecture (L):** 1 Credit
* **1 Hour Tutorial (T):** 1 Credit
* **1 Hour Practical (P):** 0.5 Credits

### 2. Program Structure & Credit Distribution
The total requirement for the degree is **169 Credits**.

### 3. Key Course Highlights
The curriculum covers fundamental and advanced mechanical engineering topics across 8 semesters:

* **Foundation:** Mathematics (Calculus, Linear Algebra), Engineering Physics, and Engineering Graphics.
* **Core Mechanics:** Engineering Mechanics, Solid Mechanics, and Fluid Mechanics.
* **Thermal Sciences:** Engineering Thermodynamics, Heat and Mass Transfer.
* **Design & Manufacturing:** Manufacturing Technology, Design of Machine Elements, and CAD/CAM.
* **Modern Integration:** Industrial IoT, Mechatronics, and Automation in Material Handling.

### 4. Course Outcomes (Example)
Upon completion of core courses like **Material Handling Systems**, students will:
1. Apply foundational principles and system approaches.
2. Solve technical problems using modern engineering techniques.
3. Master the design of integrated material handling systems.
